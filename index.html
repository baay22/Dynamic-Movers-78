Six Flags Great Adventure

Along with the Disney World, Six Flags is one of the most famous amusement parks in the USA and around the world. This amusement park, in particular, Six Flags Great Adventure, is located in Jackson, New Jersey. Philadelphia and New York City share approximately the same distance to this amusement park. With the capital of New Jersey located close by, we can freely say that Six Flags Great Adventure can rest assured that knowing that they will not be lacking clients any time soon.

Since 2012, when Wild Safari Animal Park was incorporated into Six Flags Great Adventure, this became the second largest amusement park in the world. The only larger one is Disney’s Animal Kingdom.

Given the fact that the park is divided into 11 differently themed areas, we confident that your family is going to be able to find something for everyone when you visit this amazing place.

Jenkinson’s Aquarium

The next stop on our list is a perfect place for all of you moving from Queens and who would like to enjoy spending your free time as close as possible to marine life. Therefore, welcome to Jenkinson’s Aquarium!

Aquariums will bring marine life closer to your kids

This large aquarium is located on the boardwalk in Point Pleasant Beach, New Jersey. Here, you will have an opportunity not only to get to know the deep-sea marine life but much more! If you happen to stumble upon a parrot or a monkey, do not be afraid! Animals such as these also make up a portion of the exhibit!

So, in the case that you are into the animal kingdom, Jenkinson’s Aquarium is the place to visit as soon as possible!

The Funplex Indoor & Outdoor Park and Waterpark spells family fun in New Jersey

Now, we are going to pay a visit to another amusement park. In the case that you like getting wet, you should make sure to plan a visit to this amazing park after moving to NJ from Staten Island as soon as possible.

The Funplex Indoor & Outdoor Park and Waterpark is located in Mount Laurel, Burlington County, New Jersey. While they have many different attractions where you can spend quality family fun in New Jersey, they do have one specialty – birthday parties!

If you are looking for the best way to make your child remember his or her birthday, then you should seriously consider throwing a birthday party at The Funplex Indoor & Outdoor Park and Waterpark. Utter family fun in New Jersey guaranteed!

iPlay America

Lastly, for all of you who are looking for super family fun in New Jersey, we would suggest visiting iPlay America.

Waterparks are great family fun!

First of all, it recreates Jersey City from the Boardwalk all the way to the concrete streets in downtown. Secondly, the admission to the complex, which spreads on 4 acres is free! Once inside, you choose which activity you would like to try and buy singular tickets for all every one of them. Once tired, you may decide to go see a movie or go for lunch at a local restaurant. In fact, everything you need for some amazing family fun in New Jersey can be found at iPlay America.
